Committee

Our dedicated committee is at the heart of organizing this event. Comprised of industry experts, seasoned professionals, and passionate volunteers, the committee is committed to delivering a remarkable experience. Each member brings unique expertise and a shared vision to ensure the success of the conference. Together, they work tirelessly to curate a program that is insightful, engaging, and beneficial for all attendees.

ABOUT
SCHEMCON-2024 marks the 20th edition of this significant gathering, jointly organized by the Department of Chemical Engineering at Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology, Jais and the Amethi Regional Centre of the Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers. The overarching theme of SCHEMCON 2024 revolves around The future of the new paradigms of Chemical Engineering.
