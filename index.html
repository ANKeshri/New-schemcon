About SCHEMCON

SCHEMCON, the Students Chemical Engineering Congress, is an annual event organized by the Students Chapter of the Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers (IIChE) under one of its Regional Centers. It serves as a pivotal platform for Chemical Engineering students to engage, learn, and immerse themselves in their field. SCHEMCON-2024 marks the 20th edition, jointly organized by the Department of Chemical Engineering at Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology, Jais, and the Amethi Regional Centre of IIChE. The theme for SCHEMCON 2024 is ‘New Paradigms of Chemical Engineering,’ promising a diverse array of perspectives and innovative ideas, with participation from industry leaders, researchers, educators, and students across disciplines.

About Organizing Institute

The Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology (RGIPT), Jais, Amethi, Uttar Pradesh, was established by the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas (MoPNG), Government of India, through an Act of Parliament in 2007. Recognized as an Institution of National Importance, RGIPT was ranked 79th in the engineering category by NIRF in 2023. It is co-promoted as an energy-specific institute by leading oil public sector units (ONGC, IOCL, OIL, GAIL, BPCL, and HPCL) along with the OIDB. The institute is associated with top international universities specializing in petroleum, petrochemicals, and energy sectors. With 7 departments and 2 centers, RGIPT offers 13 UG, 6 PG, and doctoral programs in STEM fields. For more information, Visit Here.

About IIChE

The Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers (IIChE) was established on May 18, 1947, at Jadavpur University, Kolkata, by Dr. Hira Lal Roy, and it has since been headquartered there. IIChE aims to promote Chemical Engineering education and foster the profession's growth. With around 29,000 members, 42 Regional Centres, and over 165 Student Chapters, IIChE is recognized by the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India, as a Scientific and Industrial Research Organization. The institute organizes the annual Indian Chemical Engineering Congress (CHEMCON) and has been hosting SCHEMCON (Students' CHEMCON) annually since 2005.

About IIChE Amethi Regional Center

The IIChE-Amethi Regional Centre (ARC) was established in December 2021 at the Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology, Jais, located in Amethi District. Currently, IIChE-ARC comprises 19 faculty members and is continually growing. The center is dedicated to advancing the Chemical Engineering profession by organizing meetings, conferences, seminars, and workshops. Additionally, refresher courses and counseling sessions for students are held to encourage scientific inquiry and innovation. For more information, Visit Here.

About MP Chary Award

The IIChE annually awards the M.P. Chary Award to an outstanding Chemical Engineer under 35 years of age, either pursuing or having recently completed a Ph.D. The recipient is required to present their research work or views on a selected topic. The award includes the M.P. Chary Medal and a prize of Rs. 10,000/-. The submission template is available Here.

Frequently Asked Questions

We've compiled a list of the most commonly asked questions to help you find the information you need quickly. If you have any further inquiries, feel free to reach out to our support team for assistance.

What is the accommodation arrangement for students?

Students will be permitted to avail the share hostel room accommodation ONLY (separately for boys and girls) on nominal accommodation fee basis. However, each student must register for the conference individually and provide the details to avail the accommodation facility.

Can I change my oral presentation to poster presentation.

Yes, students can submit a request through email to change their oral presentation to poster presentation is required. The same will be converted after the consideration by the committee members.

How to prepare for the oral presentation and poster?

In the oral presentations, 8 mins will be given to the presenting authors for the presentation followed by 2 mins of question answers. In poster presentations authors have to make a poster and the poster will be displayed in the exhibition hall. The authors will have to explain the content of their poster to the judges on their turn. The templates for the oral presentations and posters will be shared in the 1st week of August 2024.

Conference proceedings details?

The abstracts will be published in the conference abstract (souvenir) booklet.

What is the registration process?

Registration fees vary based on the participant's category (student, professional, IIChE member, non-member, etc.). Detailed fee information is available on the registration page of the website.
