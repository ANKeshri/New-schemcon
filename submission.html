Submission Guidelines

Welcome to the SCHEMCON 2024 submission page. To ensure a smooth submission process for your abstract, please adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Submit your abstract in the provided format.
  • Ensure your abstract is concise and meets the word limit requirements.
  • Include all required sections as outlined in the submission guidelines.
  • Submit your abstract before the deadline to ensure timely review.

For any queries or further assistance, please refer to the contact information provided on the conference website.

Submissions & Publication Guidelines

Please ensure that your submission adheres to the specified format and guidelines for abstracts. All abstracts must be submitted in PDF format and should follow the provided template closely. Ensure clarity and conciseness in your writing, and adhere to the submission deadlines to facilitate a smooth review process. For detailed instructions, refer to the submission guidelines document linked on the conference website.

Conference Track

Discover the diverse range of topics that will be covered during SCHEMCON 2024. Each track is designed to provide in-depth insights into the latest research, innovations, and industry trends in Chemical Engineering, fostering engaging discussions among participants.

  • 01 - Energy Transition and Renewable Energy Technology
  • 02 - Hydrogen Energy Technology: Harnessing the Power of Hydrogen
  • 03 - Bio-Based Energy Innovations: Bioenergy and Biofuels
  • 04 - Waste to Energy Conversion for a Greener Future
  • 05 - Energy Storage Solutions: Beyond the Batteries
  • 06 - Advances in Material Science and Engineering
  • 07 - Process Modelling, Simulation, and Optimization
  • 08 - Electrochemical Systems and Processes: Towards Zero-Emission Solutions
  • 09 - Smart Chemical Engineering: Application of IoT, Data Analytics, and AI/ML
  • 10 - Polymers and Petrochemicals
  • 11 - Green Separation and Purification Technologies
  • 12 - Chemical Engineering in Healthcare
  • 13 - Rethinking Conventional Energy: Advancements in Conventional Fuels and Chemicals
  • 14 - Sustainable Practices in Agriculture, Environment, and Pollution Control
  • 15 - Safety, Hazard, and Sustainable Practices in Chemical Engineering
  • 16 - Green Technologies for Net Zero Discharge and Water Resources Management

ABOUT
SCHEMCON-2024 marks the 20th edition of this significant gathering, jointly organized by the Department of Chemical Engineering at Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology, Jais and the Amethi Regional Centre of the Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers. The overarching theme of SCHEMCON 2024 revolves around The future of the new paradigms of Chemical Engineering.
